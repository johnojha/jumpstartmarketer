It's important that you use the proper class names. Both functionality and styling depend on it. The slider attributes should be set. A slider without the slider attributes will work, but with the default settings (shown above). The default position for the slider is specified in the style attribute, as left: [position in pixels]px for horizontal sliders, and top: [position in pixels from top]px.
